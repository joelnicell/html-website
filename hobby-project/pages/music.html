Music

Music is one my main passions, with my interests spanning multiple genres and disciplines.

Piano

I've played piano since I was 8 years old. I grew up learning classical, until I went to unversity, where I studied Jazz piano

Since then, I've shifted my focus towards creating meditative compositions. I'm inspired by pianists / composers such as:

  • Nils Frahms

    Classical and electronic composer from Berlin

  • Olafur Arnolds

    Ambient and electronic composer from Iceland

  • Jon Hopkins

    Electronic composer and producer from England

Production

I produce and engineer EDM, electronic, pop and RnB music. What started as a hobby turned into a job during covid.

During lockdown, I took the time and opportunity to turn my room into a studio. This included:

  1. Measuring the acoustics of the room
  2. Researching and buying soundproof material
  3. Installing fixtures in my room
